Money Heist

Plot

"Money Heist" follows a criminal mastermind who goes by the alias "The Professor" and recruits a group of eight criminals to carry out an ambitious heist on the Royal Mint of Spain. Their plan involves infiltrating the mint, printing billions of euros, and escaping without getting caught. As the heist unfolds, tensions rise among the group and unforeseen challenges threaten to derail their meticulously planned operation.

Cast & Crew

Creator: Álex Pina

Main Cast: Úrsula Corberó, Álvaro Morte, Itziar Ituño, Pedro Alonso, Alba Flores, Miguel Herrán, Jaime Lorente, Esther Acebo
